Howdy Kanjoya,

I'm interested in language. Emotionally rich content is important, we currently don't have the tools to fully understand speech. This becomes increasingly important in an age of voice-recognition, targeted advertising, and shared online experiences.

That is why I worked at Nuance Communications, and it is why I want to work at Kanjoya. At Nuance, I built systems that could communicate with humans. This was fascinating to me, as a Systems Design Engineer, the most interesting system to me is the interaction between the human the the computer. I want to continue this, I want to further the understanding of natural language in humans through the use of Big Data and statistical methods and use it to help and shape the world around us. It won't be easy, since natural language processing is such a complex craft that relies on so many different fields, ranging from linguistics, to psychology, to good ol' fashion coding. There's a lot of work to be done, and that's why I want in. I want to be part of the next stage of development in human understanding
